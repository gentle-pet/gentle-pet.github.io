Pet-care apps are increasingly subscription-based: you pay every month or year, create an account, and your pet's medical history lives in someone else's cloud. If you'd rather pay once and keep your data on your own device, your options narrow fast. Here's an honest look at what's available in 2026.

What to look for in a no-subscription pet health app

  • Pricing model — one-time purchase vs. recurring subscription (monthly/yearly).
  • Offline & privacy — does it work without internet, and does your data stay on your device?
  • Account required? — do you have to sign up and put records in the cloud?
  • Multiple pets — is there a paywall on a second or third pet?
  • Core records — vaccinations, deworming, vet visits, weight, medications, reminders, PDF export.
