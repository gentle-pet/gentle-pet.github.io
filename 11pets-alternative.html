Why people look for an 11pets alternative

11pets is a well-established, account-based pet-care app with a broad feature set. People who go searching for an alternative usually aren't unhappy with it as a product — they're reacting to a model they'd rather avoid. The recurring reasons we see in this search:

  • Subscription fatigue. You already pay monthly for streaming, storage and a dozen other apps. Adding another recurring fee — just to keep your dog's vaccine dates — feels like too much.
  • "I want it to work without internet." At the vet, on a hike, in a basement clinic with no signal — owners want their records to open instantly, offline.
  • "Do I have to make an account?" Some people simply don't want to create yet another login, or put their pet's medical history into someone else's cloud.
  • Wanting to own the data. If an app shuts down or a plan changes, owners want their records to still live on their own phone, exportable as a file.

Note: we describe 11pets only by its general, well-known model (an account-based pet-care app with its own plans). We deliberately do not quote 11pets' specific prices or features here, because they change — confirm the current details on 11pets' official site.

What an 11pets alternative should give you

If the reason you're switching is "no subscription, more privacy," then the alternative is only worth it if it actually delivers on those points. The checklist:

  • Pay-once pricing — a one-time purchase, not a monthly or yearly plan.
  • Offline-first — opens and saves records with no internet connection.
  • No mandatory account — you can use it without signing up.
  • Local data — records stored on your device, not collected or shared.
  • The records that matter — vaccinations, deworming, vet visits, weight, allergies, medications, reminders, microchip ID, and an export you can hand to a vet.
