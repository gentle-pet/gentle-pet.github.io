This page is specifically about running a whole household of animals from one place — what that looks like day to day, and where GentlePet's model actually helps.

The per-pet paywall problem

Plenty of pet apps are free or cheap for one animal, then ask for more money the moment you add a second. For someone with a single pet that's fine. For a multi-pet home it adds up fast and turns a simple records app into a recurring cost that scales with how many animals you love.

GentlePet's Premium is a flat one-time unlock. Add pet number two, five or ten — the price doesn't change, and there's no monthly fee waiting in the background. The free tier lets you keep one pet (with up to 30 photos and 5 reminders) so you can try the workflow before paying anything.

What "one app for every pet" actually looks like

Each animal gets its own profile. You switch between them with a tap; you're never merging unrelated histories or guessing which vaccine belonged to which cat.

Multi-pet questions

Does GentlePet charge per pet?

No. The free tier covers one pet; Premium unlocks unlimited pets for a single one-time $4.99 purchase, with no per-pet fee and no subscription.

How many pets can I add?

The free tier supports one pet. With Premium there's no fixed cap or paywall on additional pets — your dogs, cats and other animals all live in the same app.

Are each pet's records kept separate?

Yes. Every animal has its own profile with its own vaccines, weight, vet visits, allergies, medications and reminders. You switch between them with a tap.

Can I export records for just one pet?

Yes — you can export a PDF medical card per pet, so you only hand the vet the records for the animal you're there about.

Do I need an account or internet for multiple pets?

No. GentlePet works fully offline with no account; all pets' records are stored locally on your phone.
